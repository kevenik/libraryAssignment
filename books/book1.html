The Great Gatsby

By F. Scott Fitzgerald

About the Book

Published in 1925, *The Great Gatsby* is a timeless tale of ambition, love, and the pursuit of the American Dream. Set in the Roaring Twenties, this classic novel captures the essence of the Jazz Age through the life of Jay Gatsby, a mysterious millionaire obsessed with the elusive Daisy Buchanan.

F. Scott Fitzgerald weaves a poignant narrative of glamour, deceit, and heartbreak, exploring themes of wealth, social class, and the disillusionment of the American Dream.
